The Boulder Code&&Coffee meets every Wednesday mornings from 9am until noon at dojo4.

While we trace our origins to a handful of Rubyists, we are open to developers of any persuasion. This includes sexual orientation, religious beliefs (or lack thereof), race, and the many diverse attributes that makes us human.

We also extends this inclusiveness to choice of operating systems, editors and languages.
